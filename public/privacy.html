Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Biblical Canvas is a local-first design tool. By default, external services are disabled until you choose to enable them. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it in compliance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

1. Data We Process

We process the content you enter (for example Bible references, verse text edits, and design settings) mostly inside your browser. Privacy choices and local configurations are stored in your browser's local storage.

If you utilize our "Share with Developer" feature, we store the generated image and its configuration settings on our server to generate a shareable link. No personal identifiable information is attached to these uploads unless explicitly included in the design itself.

2. External Services (Optional)

If you opt-in to enable external services, requests may be sent to third-party providers such as Unsplash, API.Bible, bible-api.com, and Google Fonts. These providers may receive your IP address, browser metadata, and request content to fulfill the service. Their respective privacy policies govern their use of your data.

3. Google Analytics

With your explicit consent, we use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about the use of our app and report on activities and trends. This helps us improve the user experience of Biblical Canvas.

The information generated by the cookie about your use of the app (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. We have enabled IP anonymization where possible. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by withdrawing your consent in the "Update Privacy Choices" section of the app.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage strictly for essential functional purposes (e.g., remembering your privacy choices and app settings). We do not use non-essential tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies without your explicit consent.

5. European Union (GDPR) & UK Users

If you are located in the EEA or the UK, we process your data based on your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) when you enable external services or share designs. You have the right to:

  • Access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (via "Update Privacy Choices" in the app).
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

6. US State Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a resident of California or another US state with similar laws, you have specific rights regarding your personal information.

  • We do not sell or "share" your personal information to third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected.
  • You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

7. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until cleared. Shared designs stored on our server are kept temporarily and may be periodically purged. We do not retain shared designs indefinitely.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at: casmedlin@wbem.org.
